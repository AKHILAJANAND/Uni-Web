Our Certificates And Online Programs For 2023

Our online Masters programme will help you develop the theoretical and practical knowledge and skills you need to stand out in computing. You have two specialist pathways to choose from: Data Analytics or Artificial Intelligence.


You’ll be taught by academics from a university which is part of the UK government’s £40m Institute of Coding for growing digital skills. You’ll learn how to use sought-after programming web languages and software, including Python, Javascript, Microsoft Azure, Oracle SQL Plus, Pandas and Python 3.Grow your knowledge to harness the power of data. On this pathway, you’ll hone your specialist data skillset, exploring machine learning methods, database technologies, data visualisation techniques, and statistical knowledge to draw insight from real business data.


Our course is 100% online, meaning you can choose when and where you study. You can gain a Masters in around two years, part time, or take longer if needed. Plus, you can also pay your fees in module-by-module instalments.
